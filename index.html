Mart's Discord Permissions Calculator

A tool that generates authorization links for your Discord bot

What is this?

If you want to invite a Discord bot to your server, you will need an authorization link. This contains the client ID of the bot, and a permissions integer. This tool calculates that permissions integer for you and gives you the authorization link you can use. Enter the client ID of the bot, select the permissions your bot needs, copy the link, and share your bot! Note: in some browsers, to show the results, you will need to type something in the client ID field after selecting the permissions.
