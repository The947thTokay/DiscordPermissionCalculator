Mart's Discord Permission Calculator

A rubbish tool that generates invite links for your Discord bot

How do I use this?

To give your bot the correct permissions on servers it is invited to, you will need to include a permission integer in the link you share:
https://discordapp.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client_id=CLIENT-ID&scope=bot&permissions=PERMISSION-INTEGER
where CLIENT-ID should be replaced by the client ID of your bot and PERMISSION-INTEGER should be replaced by the permission integer you can calculate with this tool.

Select the permissions you want to give to your bot, or choose it to be an administrator (which will allow everything). You can put this in the link above, or give a client ID and copy the link.
