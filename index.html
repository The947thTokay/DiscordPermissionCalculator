Mart's Discord Permission Calculator

A rubbish tool that generates authorization links for your Discord bot

What is this?

If you want to invite a Discord bot to your server, you will need an authorization link. This contains the client ID of the bot, and a permissions integer. This tool calculates that permissions integer for you and gives you the authorization link you can use. You can enter the client ID and select the permissions your bot needs. Finally, you can copy the link and share your bot!

Select the permissions you want to give to your bot, or choose it to be an administrator (which will allow everything). You can put this in the link above, or give a client ID and copy the link.
